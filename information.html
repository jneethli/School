About Chess Emporium

The Chess Emporium is the premier provider of scholastic chess classes and tournaments in the Phoenix area. Over the past 20 years, in excess of 70,000 students have been trained by our passionate and dedicated coaches. Our four large commercial chess centers in Phoenix, Scottsdale, and Tempe provide all aspiring students access to our products and services (regardless of the school which they attend).

Program Details

The Chess4Life program is divided into six levels from beginner to advanced. Prospective students are given an assessment to determine which level is best for them. Students graduate to higher levels as they demonstrate mastery of core concepts.


Summer Break Chess Camps Fun, structured chess camps for beginner to early-intermediate students (approximately 100–800 USCF) at our Scottsdale and Tempe locations. Camps provide a positive environment where students build friendships, strengthen their minds through fun learning, and enjoy a healthy alternative to endless holiday screen time.

Rating Explanation

In chess, students will earn a rating, which usally starts at 100 and can climb as high as 3000 or more. Below are the explanations for how each rating reflects the players strength:

Level Rating Explanation
Beginner 0-1000 Basic Understanding of piece movement and checkmating.
Intermediate 1000-1900 Strong chess understanding and memorization of several openings. Clear fundamental skill.
Professional 1900-2800 At this level, chess players may gain titles such as intermediate master or grand master. Usually top 1% of the world.
